About Gyanmanjari Open Source Program

Gyanmanjari Open Source Program is a one-week-long Open-Source Program conducted every summer by the GMIU. With constant efforts, participants contribute to numerous projects under the extreme guidance of skilled mentors over these months. With such exposure, students begin to contribute to real-world projects from the comfort of their homes.
